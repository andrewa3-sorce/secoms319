Trading Card Game Catalog

Magic the Gathering is one of the first trading card games to be released. The game was released in 1993 by the same company that created Dungeons and Dragons, Wizards of the Coast.

Yu-Gi-Oh! was released in 1999. This trading card game, unlike Magic, was adapted from a TV show of the same title, where the show focuses on characters who play a version of the game.

1999

Pokemon was released in 1996. This game, much like Yu-Gi-Oh! was adapted from a TV show. However, unlike Yu-Gi-Oh!, the Pokemon TV show does not feature a game like the trading card game. Instead, the card game just references characters and items that appear in the show.

Flesh and Blood is one of the newest of these 5 card games, being released in 2019. This game, much like Magic the Gathering, is a stand-alone card game that wasn't adapted from another IP. With a rise in interest in trading card games, Legendary Story Studios capitalized by releasing Flesh and Blood which has quickly become popular in the TCG scene.

Digimon is yet another game that was created based on an existing TV show. Originally released in 2000 as "Digi-Battle", the game was around for 1 year before the contract between Upper Deck and Bandai expired. In 2020, however, the card game has been rereleased as Digimon Card Game.
